About



The Sovrin DevExchange was built to allow companies to easily locate developers with the skills needed to work on their projects. The Sovrin DevExchange helps reduce some of the overhead in that relationship and provides the means for company and developer to find each other.



How We Got Here

The Sovrin Devexchange began with the open source code base that is the base of the BCDevExchange app. Our new adaptation of that code base is in GitHub and is an open source project intending to provide similar services as the original, but to a much wider audience.

Who We Are

We are a team of developers hoping to provide a means for identity minded companies and developers to join tegether and produce something incredible!

How You Can Help

The Sovrin Foundation cannot make this happen all on their own. Experience tells us that collaboration between internal and external contributors leads us to the best ideas. We welcome all who would like to contribute in any way to the success of the Sovrin DevExchange project.
